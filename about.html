About Us

FemPrish: Empowering Equality in India. FemPrish is a non-profit organization challenging negative perceptions of feminism. Focusing on India, they promote gender equality, combat violence, and improve access to education and healthcare. Through advocacy and collaboration, FemPrish strives for a more inclusive society where everyone can thrive.

FemPrish Mission:

  • Redefine Feminism: Challenging negative connotations, FemPrish aims to redefine feminism as a force for positive change and inclusivity.

  • Promote Gender Equality: Dedicated to promoting gender equality in India, FemPrish works to eliminate barriers and create equal opportunities for all individuals

  • Improve Access: By advocating for improved access to education and healthcare, FemPrish seeks to empower individuals and bridge gender disparities

  • Foster Collaboration: FemPrish collaborates with communities, governmental bodies, and other organizations to drive systemic change and create a more inclusive society

FemPrish Team:

Comprised of passionate individuals, the FemPrish team is dedicated to driving change and promoting gender equality in India. From activists to educators, their diverse backgrounds bring a wealth of knowledge and experience to the organization. United by a shared vision, the team works tirelessly to challenge stereotypes, advocate for policy changes, and empower individuals. With their collective expertise and unwavering commitment, the FemPrish team is at the forefront of creating a more inclusive and equal society for all in India.
